          Welcome to Launchbot

          A startpage to open collections of websites with one click. It’s like restoring tabs, but anywhere. Things to know:

          1. Open the Settings to customize your collections, manage plugins, and to adjust options.
          2. Keyboard shortcuts: '1' to '9' (open set), 's' or '/' (search).
          3. You need to allow pop-ups from Launchbot. Your browser should prompt you.
          4. Your data is only stored in your browser’s localStorage. It will remain there until you manually delete it.
          5. Launchbot is free and open source. It’s easy to self-host.
          6. You can create your own embedded default config for all devices, so that you don’t have to set things up twice. Learn more about it on GitHub.
